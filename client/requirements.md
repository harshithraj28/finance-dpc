## Packages
recharts | Dashboard analytics charts and data visualization
html2canvas | Required for generating PNG exports of reports
jspdf | Required for generating PDF exports of reports
date-fns | Human-readable date formatting

## Notes
- Replit Auth is used for authentication. Landing page directs to /api/login and /api/logout.
- Wouter is used for routing.
- Using html2canvas and jspdf for client-side report exports.
